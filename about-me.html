About Me

I don't own any suits, I photoshopped this one.

Hey there! This is the about me page, and this is the part where I write a few cheesy paragraph about myself. It's a little long I'm sorry, but there's just so much to include!
My name is Alec Cromer. I am 28 year old full-time Senior Software Engineer, and Masters graduate from Texas A&M in Visualization.

The best feeling in the world to me is when I make something that others can enjoy, and using my love of computers I focused on media software as a way to express myself. I grew up editing gaming YouTube videos, and then began coding to create websites and random programs back in high school. I even started a comic dubbing YouTube channel where I managed over a dozen artists, voice actors, editors, and musicians, and our videos helped entertain over half a million people! 

I went to the University of North Georgia to get a Computer Science degree. Outside of class I learned more about 3D animation and that I could use my love for software to make games, animations, and movies. Using this, I self taught myself how to 3D model, draw, and rig. Being in a Computer Science program meant that I had limited access to art classes which meant I learned most of my skills independently. I occasionally write scripts, and work on small games in Unity or Unreal Engine. My job keeps me busy so it's been hard to create and finish on these projects.

The world is full of artists, and I've learned just how hard it is to express yourself through digital media. It takes hundreds of hours to study color, modeling, animation, rigging, lighting...etc, and each piece of software is different in how it functions. My intention was to go back to school to get a better understanding of the deeper fundamentals of computer graphics and art to then improve the software and tools we use, to make it easier and faster to create digital art so that everyone can show their creativity to the world.
Now that I have completed my degree at Texas A&M, I'm one step closer to my dream of helping others create the captivating animations and games that I grew up to love!
